Privacy Policy

Raqeeb – رقيب  ·  Last updated: April 18, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Mohamed Elgaili Ali ("we", "our", or "us") handles information when you use the Raqeeb – رقيب Android application ("the App"), available on Google Play.

1. Information We Collect

a) Data you enter into the App (stored locally on your device)

  • Daily prayer records (whether each prayer was performed, time, congregation, voluntary prayers)
  • Daily check-in data (fasting, Quran reading, pages read, Quran memorisation progress)
  • Personal goals (fasting targets, Quran and Hifz goals, reading plans)
  • Hifz (Quran memorisation) planner settings and progress
  • Book reading records and library content (PDF files you import)
  • App preferences and tracker settings

All of this data is stored only on your device by default. Nothing is sent to any server unless you explicitly enable the optional cloud backup feature.

b) Account data (only if you create an optional account)

  • Email address
  • Password (stored securely by our authentication provider, Supabase — never in plaintext)
  • A cloud backup of your app data (same items listed above), stored on Supabase servers

c) Location data

The App requests your device location solely to calculate accurate Islamic prayer times for your position. Location is used in real time and is never stored on our servers. Your last known coordinates may be cached locally on your device to show prayer times without repeating the location request. You may deny location permission; you can then enter your city manually instead.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To display your prayer records, tracking history, and goals within the App
  • To calculate daily prayer times based on your location
  • To send local notification reminders for prayer times (if you enable them)
  • To back up and restore your data across devices (only if you use the optional cloud backup)

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose beyond providing the App's features.

5. Permissions Explained

  • Location (fine & coarse) — only to calculate prayer times for your area; never stored on servers.
  • Internet — to fetch prayer times and for optional cloud backup.
  • Post notifications — to send local prayer-time reminders (only if you enable them).
  • Receive boot completed — to reschedule notification alarms after the device restarts.
  • Wake lock — to ensure notification alarms fire reliably.

6. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

7. Security

Passwords are never stored in plaintext — they are managed entirely by Supabase using industry-standard hashing. Cloud backup data is transmitted over HTTPS. Local app data is stored in the private app storage on your device, inaccessible to other apps.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page will reflect any changes. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
