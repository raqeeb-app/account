Delete Your Account

You can permanently delete your Raqeeb account and all associated data at any time.

What gets deleted

Deleting your account removes the following permanently:

  • Your authentication credentials (email & password)
  • All cloud backup data linked to your account
  • Your account profile on our servers

Data stored locally on your device (prayer records, Quran tracking, etc.) is not affected and remains on your phone.

⚠ This action is permanent and cannot be undone. Your cloud backup will be lost.

How to delete in-app

  1. Open the Raqeeb app
  2. Tap the settings icon (⚙) on the main screen
  3. Select Account Settings
  4. Scroll down and tap Delete Account
  5. Confirm the action in the dialog

Need help or can't access the app?

If you're unable to sign in or need us to delete your account manually, email us and we'll process it within 7 days.
