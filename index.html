About this page

This is the official account management page for Raqeeb – رقيب, published on Google Play by Mog Academy. Here you can delete your account or remove your cloud backup data at any time.

How to delete your account

You can request account deletion in two ways:

  1. In the app — open Settings → Account Settings → Delete Account and confirm.
  2. On this page — sign in below, then tap Delete My Account.
  3. By email — contact us at support@raqeeb.app and we will process your request within 7 days.

What data is deleted & what is kept

DataOn account deletionOn cloud data deletion only
Sign-in credentials (email & password) Deleted immediately Kept
Cloud backup (prayer records, Quran tracking, goals, settings) Deleted immediately Deleted immediately
Local app data on your device Not affected Not affected
Anonymised crash & analytics data (if any) Retained up to 90 days Retained up to 90 days

There is no additional retention period for account or backup data — deletion is permanent and immediate upon confirmation.
